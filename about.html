About Crescent Compliance

We are a dynamic and innovative firm whose main goal is to provide, develop and promote compliance in your organisation so that you can focus on your core business with piece of mind. Our aim is to make our culture one driven by excellence and innovation.

We adapt fluidly to different client business models and a changing regulatory environment, whilst maintaining a strong core of integrity, excellence and innovation.

We are proud to be a level 1 B-BBEE black female-owned business.

CEO Kashnie Naidoo

Kashnie has worked in the stockbroking industry for 20 years at various stockbrokers. The last ten years were spent at the JSE – in Market Regulation. She is passionate about both the industry and compliance within the industry.

She is a member of the South African Institute of Stockbrokers, a member of the South African Institute of Financial Markets Stockbrokers as well as an Affiliate Member of the Compliance Institute of South Africa.
