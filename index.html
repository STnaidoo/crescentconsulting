Compliance Services

Crescent Compliance Solutions aims to provide a bespoke compliance offering to augment your organisations compliance to a level of excellence. We help our clients transform and increase the efficiency and effectiveness of compliance.

We do not seek to replace compliance in the business but to augment the function. Through developing and executing a strategy for the management of compliance whilst enhancing quality and increasing the value obtained from your compliance activities.

Vision

To provide, develop and promote compliance excellence

Mission

To provide organisations with financial markets compliance solutions to augment compliance structures within regulated organisations

What We Offer

Consulting

We offer consulting services designed to meet your compliance regulatory needs

Advisory

We provide expert guidance to organisations regarding applicable legislation and exchange rules

Policies and Procedures

We draft policies and procedures to ensure that your organisation's compliance obligations are met

New Compliance Function

We assist your internal compliance with setting up a new compliance function.

External Compliance Audits

We conduct audit reviews to assess your internal controls to identify deficiencies proactively and ensure low risk of non-compliance

Training

We offer customised training to promote compliance within your organisation across various legislation
